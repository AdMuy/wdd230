Site purpose:

The purpose of the site is giving an opportunity to the business, small and big from our local area, to get exposure to the potential clients
and strength our community. We know that is hard to make connections and with the rapid avance of technology we can lose many customers with a single click.
That's the reason why we have a dedicated directory with the most up-to-date information of our member businesses, we will also have a page dedicated
to any entrepreneur who wants to join our chamber and enjoy many benefits.
As our community is the most important part of this project, we also have a Discover page, where we can get to know more about our local area and
rich history we usually tend to forget.
Lastly, we will also have a dedicated page to Contact Us, there you will find more information about our current officers and send any feedback or question you may have.
